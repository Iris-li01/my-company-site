Energy-Efficient

Featuring DC inverter technology, this pump is 20% to 30% more energy-efficient compared to standard 110/220V non-inverter pumps, resulting in long-term power savings and lower operating costs.

Ultra-Quiet

Equipped with sine wave drive and a 6-slot stator, the motor operates more smoothly, with less vibration and lower noise than traditional 2-slot motors, ensuring truly quiet and stable performance.

Long-Lasting Durability

Featuring a brushless motor with advanced 3-phase intelligent drive, the pump adjusts speed and power by regulating the PWM duty cycle, offering a significantly longer service life compared to traditional AC pumps.

No Electric Leakage Risk

The pump operates on 24V DC low voltage, completely eliminating the risk of electric shock to users. (Note: Under normal environmental conditions, the safety extra-low voltage permitted for continuous contact is 36V.)

Tool-Free Disassembly

Designed for user convenience, the pump can be quickly disassembled and assembled by hand, reducing maintenance costs. This makes cleaning and routine maintenance faster, easier, and more efficient.

Ceramic Shaft

The pump utilizes a high-quality, wear-resistant ceramic shaft. With its high hardness, low friction coefficient, non-corrosive nature, and acid-alkali resistance, it effectively reduces the failure rate and enhances overall reliability and service life, making it ideal for 24/7 continuous operation.

WiFi Smart Control

Supports WiFi smart connectivity, allowing you to remotely control the pump's on/off status, adjust flow levels, and switch operating modes via a mobile app. Stay in full control of your pump's operation from anywhere at any time for a smarter and more convenient experience.

Wide Voltage Input

The power adapter supports a wide voltage range of AC100-240V 50/60Hz, making it compatible with global power standards. It effectively withstands voltage fluctuations, ensuring stable pump operation and an extended lifespan.

Soft Start Function

Upon startup, the motor accelerates smoothly and gradually to the set speed, preventing instant voltage surges that could damage the controller. This also reduces water flow impact on the pipelines, protecting them from bursts and leaks for safer operation.

Power-Off Memory

In the event of a power outage, the pump automatically remembers and restores the previous speed and working mode upon restart. This eliminates the need for manual readjustment, offering greater convenience and ease of use.

Drought Protection

The controller features an intelligent detection program. If a dry-running state is detected three consecutive times, the pump will automatically stop operating. This effectively prevents the pump from running dry and burning out, significantly enhancing its lifespan and operational safety.

Over-Voltage Protection

Equipped with automatic over-voltage protection, the controller immediately activates its safeguard mechanism when an abnormal voltage surge occurs. This prevents damage to the controller and motor from high voltage, ensuring more stable and reliable operation.

Under-Voltage Protection

The controller also supports under-voltage monitoring. When the voltage drops too low or the power adapter malfunctions, it automatically triggers an ERR error code and shuts down. This prevents the equipment from operating under faulty conditions, providing greater peace of mind.

Stall Protection

The controller is equipped with an intelligent stall detection program. If the pump becomes jammed by debris or scale buildup, it will If the pump becomes jammed and attempt to restart up to 5 times to self-clear minor blockages. If all 5 attempts fail, the system will automatically shut down to protect the pump from burning out.

Stall/Overload Protection

Featuring a built-in overload protection program, the system immediately triggers an error alarm and activates protection when abnormal load is detected due to pipeline blockages or excessive internal scaling. This prevents motor overload and burnout, ensuring safer and more reliable operation.

Freshwater & Saltwater Compatibility

The pump body contains no metal components and is resistant to saltwater corrosion, ensuring stable operation in both freshwater and marine environments. This offers wider applicability and an extended service life.

20 Adjustable Flow Levels

Featuring 1 to 20 precise flow adjustment levels, the pump is easy to operate and meets various water flow and circulation needs for different applications.

Two Operating Modes

The constant flow mode ensures a stable water supply, while the feeding mode intelligently pauses operation to avoid disturbing your fish, providing a more user-friendly experience.
